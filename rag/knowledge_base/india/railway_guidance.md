# Indian Railway Journey Guidance

## Reservation status
A railway booking may be confirmed, reservation-against-cancellation, or waitlisted. A waitlisted ticket is not a guaranteed seat. Verify the current PNR status, train number, travel date, boarding station, coach, and departure time with an official railway source before travel.

## Cancellation
IRCTC cancellation eligibility and deductions depend on ticket status, class, time remaining before departure, chart preparation, and whether a TDR is required. Use the current IRCTC cancellation flow for an actual refund calculation. Commute OS may summarize the rule but must never claim that a ticket was cancelled or refunded unless its transaction tool succeeds.

## Operational information
Platforms, delays, diversions, coach positions, seat availability, and train-running status are time-sensitive. They must come from an approved live railway tool. Durable RAG guidance cannot establish that a particular train is currently running or available.
