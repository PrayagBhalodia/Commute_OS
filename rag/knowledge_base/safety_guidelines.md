# Travel Safety Guidelines

## Personal safety
Use recognized pickup points, verify vehicle and driver details, and avoid sharing sensitive account information. Keep emergency contacts available. At night, prefer well-lit transfer areas and avoid isolated unofficial pickup locations.

## Operational safety
Follow operator, station, airport, and local authority instructions. Do not bypass security controls or carry prohibited goods. Weather, road closure, and emergency information is time-sensitive and must come from a live authoritative source.

## System safety
DMOS must ask for explicit consent before booking. The conversational layer cannot claim a booking, payment, refund, or rebooking succeeded unless the corresponding deterministic tool returned a successful result.
