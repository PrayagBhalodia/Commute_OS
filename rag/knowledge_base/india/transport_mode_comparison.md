# Indian Transport Mode Comparison

## Road
Cabs provide door-to-door convenience and luggage handling but are sensitive to traffic, pickup delays, tolls, and dynamic prices. Buses can reduce cost but use fixed stops and schedules. Autos can suit shorter urban legs where service and local rules permit.

## Rail and metro
Intercity rail can offer useful space and city-centre access, while metro can avoid road congestion. Both require station access, platform navigation, current operating times, and last-mile planning. Booking status matters for reserved rail services.

## Flight
Flights can reduce line-haul time over long distances, but a fair comparison includes airport transfers, check-in, security, boarding, baggage reclaim, and disruption risk. Rank door-to-door journeys rather than comparing only time spent inside the vehicle.
