# Airline and Multimodal Baggage Guidance

## Allowance
Airline cabin and checked-baggage allowances depend on the operating carrier, route, cabin, and fare family. Rail, metro, and bus operators can impose different size, weight, screening, and prohibited-item rules. Verify the allowance attached to the selected service instead of relying on a general baseline.

## Special items
Declare oversized, fragile, sporting, medical, battery-powered, or valuable items before booking. Dangerous-goods restrictions can apply to liquids, sharp objects, compressed gases, flammable material, and batteries. Assistive devices and medicines may follow separate processes.

## Transfers
For separate tickets, baggage may need to be collected and checked again. Add sufficient time for reclaim, terminal transfer, check-in, and security. Live excess-baggage prices are operator data and must not be inferred from RAG.
