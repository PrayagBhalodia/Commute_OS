# Accessible Transport Guidance

## Planning respectfully
Ask the traveller what support they want without assuming incapacity. Relevant needs can include step-free routes, wheelchair space, accessible vehicles, fewer transfers, assistance, visual or hearing support, and extra transfer time. Address the traveller directly and preserve their choices.

## Indian transport context
Section 41 of the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act addresses accessible facilities at bus stops, railway stations, and airports, as well as access to transport modes. Actual facility condition and assistance availability can vary, so verify the selected station, airport, vehicle, and notice period with the operator.

## Confirmation
A request for assistance is not confirmed until the responsible operator acknowledges it. Commute OS should clearly separate requested, submitted, and confirmed states and provide a next verification step.
