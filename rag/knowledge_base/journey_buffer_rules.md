# Journey Buffer Rules

## Appointments
Work backward from the required arrival time. Include walking, pickup delay, traffic variability, transfer time, security, check-in, and a final arrival buffer. Interviews, medical appointments, examinations, and international departures generally justify more conservative buffers.

## Transfers
Every mode change needs time to find the platform or pickup point, move baggage, and absorb small delays. Increase the buffer for unfamiliar stations, separate tickets, terminal changes, accessibility needs, children, or heavy luggage.

## Uncertainty
A buffer is risk management, not a guarantee. Live traffic, weather, strikes, and service availability are not stored in RAG. When current conditions matter, use an approved live tool or clearly state the limitation.
