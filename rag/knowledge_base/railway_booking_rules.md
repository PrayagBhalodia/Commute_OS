# Railway Booking Rules

## Booking status
Rail bookings can be confirmed, reservation-against-cancellation, or waitlisted depending on the operator. A waitlisted result is not a guaranteed seat. Travellers must check the current status and operator rules before boarding.

## Identity and boarding
The passenger details on a reservation should match the required identity document. Boarding station, train number, date, coach, and departure time must be verified. Platform information is operational data and should be obtained from a live railway source rather than RAG.

## Changes and cancellation
Name changes, date changes, partial cancellations, and refunds are governed by the operator and ticket type. Charges and eligibility can change. DMOS may explain general policy but must use deterministic booking tools for simulated transactions and must not invent a cancellation outcome.
