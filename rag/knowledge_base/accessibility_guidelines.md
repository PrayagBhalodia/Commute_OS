# Accessibility Guidelines

## Planning
Ask about mobility, visual, hearing, cognitive, and communication needs without making assumptions. Allow additional transfer time and minimize unnecessary mode changes. Confirm whether step-free access, wheelchair space, assistance, or accessible vehicles are required.

## Operator assistance
Airports, railways, and some transit operators may require advance notice for assistance. Availability and notice periods are operator-specific and must be verified from a current source. A request for assistance is not confirmed until the operator acknowledges it.

## Respect and autonomy
Address the traveller directly, preserve choice, and explain tradeoffs clearly. Do not treat a companion as the decision-maker unless the traveller asks. Safety guidance should not be used to deny reasonable travel options.
