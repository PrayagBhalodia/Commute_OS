# Metro Rules

## Entry and fares
Metro tickets or smart cards are normally required before entering paid areas. Fare products and operating hours differ by city and can change, so current details must be checked with the operator. Keep the ticket or token until exit where required.

## Baggage and conduct
Passengers should keep aisles and doors clear, follow screening instructions, and avoid prohibited or hazardous items. Large baggage can be restricted during busy periods or by local operator rules. Priority seating and accessible spaces must remain available to passengers who need them.

## Last-mile planning
Metro stations may have multiple exits and may not be directly connected to the destination. Plans should include walking, accessibility constraints, and a last-mile mode where necessary. Service shutdowns and last-train times are live information and are not stored in this knowledge base.
