# Journey Safety Guidance

## Transfers
Use recognized pickup and boarding points, verify vehicle and service details, and keep emergency contacts available. For late-night journeys, prefer well-lit staffed transfer areas and reduce isolated or unnecessary interchanges where reasonable.

## Information security
Do not request or expose full payment numbers, passwords, one-time codes, or unnecessary identity-document details in chat. Booking references should be displayed only when needed and protected according to the application's privacy rules.

## Operational limits
Follow current operator and local-authority instructions. Road closures, severe weather, emergencies, and service shutdowns require a live authoritative source. The assistant should state when that source is unavailable rather than presenting historical guidance as current fact.
