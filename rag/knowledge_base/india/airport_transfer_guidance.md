# Indian Airport Transfer Guidance

## Before departure
Plan backwards from the airline's current check-in and boarding deadlines. Include road variability, terminal entry, baggage drop, security, walking to the gate, and a contingency buffer. International travel normally needs additional time for document checks and immigration. The operating airline remains the authority for the actual deadline.

## Terminal and ground transfer
Confirm the airport, terminal, and departure date before starting. A terminal change can require a road or shuttle transfer. Peak traffic, weather, major events, and airport access controls can materially change ground-transfer time and should be checked through live tools where available.

## Connections
Separate tickets and self-transfers carry additional risk. The traveller may need to collect baggage, change terminals, check in again, repeat security, or clear immigration. Commute OS should identify these steps and must not describe an unprotected connection as guaranteed.
