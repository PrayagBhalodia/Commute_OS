# Indian Metro Journey Guidance

## Tickets and entry
Metro fares, ticket products, screening rules, first and last train times, and operating hours vary by city and can change. Verify current information with the city operator. Keep the token, ticket, QR credential, or smart card available until the journey is complete where the operator requires it.

## Transfers and baggage
Allow time for security, vertical circulation, interchange walking, and finding the correct platform or station exit. Large baggage may be inconvenient or restricted by local rules. Travellers who require step-free access should verify lift availability and accessible entry gates for the intended stations.

## Last mile
A station name does not guarantee that the destination is at the station entrance. Journey options should include walking distance, the correct exit, accessibility constraints, and a suitable first-mile or last-mile leg.
